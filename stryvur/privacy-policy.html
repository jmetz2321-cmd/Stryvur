Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stryvur ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Stryvur mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email address (or Apple's anonymized relay email). We use this solely to create and manage your account.

Health & Fitness Data: With your permission, we access Apple Health data (workouts, activity, nutrition) to personalize your fitness experience. This data is processed on-device and never sold to third parties.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data (e.g. features used, session length) to improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the Stryvur experience
  • To generate AI-powered workout and coaching recommendations
  • To manage your subscription and account
  • To improve our App and services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Apple: For Sign in with Apple authentication and in-app subscriptions
  • AI Services: Anonymized fitness data may be processed by AI providers to generate coaching recommendations

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Stryvur is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Authentication is handled entirely through Apple's secure Sign in with Apple system.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.
