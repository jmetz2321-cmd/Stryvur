Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Stryvur → Cancel Subscription.

How do I manage my free trial?

Your 7-day free trial begins when you first subscribe. You can cancel before day 7 with no charge. After the trial, billing begins automatically.

My workout data isn't syncing with Apple Health

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Stryvur and make sure all permissions are enabled.
